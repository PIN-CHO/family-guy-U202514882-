MEG GRIFFIN

Meg Griffin is a main character in the animated television series Family Guy. She is the teenage daughter of Peter and Lois Griffin and the older sister of Chris and Stewie Griffin. Meg is often portrayed as an awkward and unpopular teenager who struggles to fit in with her peers. She is frequently the target of ridicule and bullying, both at school and within her own family. Despite this, Meg is shown to be a kind-hearted and caring individual who desires acceptance and love from those around her. Throughout the series, Meg's character is used to explore themes of adolescence, self-esteem, and the challenges of growing up.
