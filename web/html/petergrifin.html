PETER GRIFFIN

Peter Griffin is the main character of the animated television series Family Guy. He is the husband of Lois Griffin and the father of Meg, Chris, and Stewie Griffin. Peter is known for his bumbling and often inappropriate behavior, as well as his love of beer and food. He works at the Pawtucket Brewery and is often seen hanging out with his friends at the Drunken Clam bar. Despite his flaws, Peter is a loving husband and father who cares deeply for his family.
