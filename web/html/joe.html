JOE SWANSON

Joe Swanson is a recurring character in the animated television series Family Guy. He is the Griffins' tough and determined neighbor, known for his role as a police officer and his distinctive wheelchair-bound appearance. Joe is characterized by his strong sense of justice, bravery, and unwavering dedication to his job, despite the challenges posed by his disability. He is also a loyal friend to Peter Griffin and the rest of the gang, often participating in their various adventures and misadventures. Throughout the series, Joe's character is used to explore themes of perseverance, friendship, and overcoming adversity.
