STEWIE GRIFFIN

Stewie Griffin is a main character in the animated television series Family Guy. He is the youngest child of Peter and Lois Griffin and the younger brother of Meg and Chris Griffin. Stewie is known for his advanced intelligence, sophisticated vocabulary, and his often diabolical plans. Despite being a baby, Stewie exhibits adult-like behavior and has a complex personality that includes a mix of innocence and malevolence. Throughout the series, Stewie's character is used to explore various comedic and dramatic storylines, often involving his interactions with his family and his attempts to achieve his ambitious goals.
