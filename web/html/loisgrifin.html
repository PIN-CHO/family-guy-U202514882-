LOIS GRIFFIN

Lois Griffin is a main character in the animated television series Family Guy. She is the wife of Peter Griffin and the mother of Meg, Chris, and Stewie Griffin. Lois is known for her strong-willed personality and her ability to keep her family in line despite Peter's often outrageous behavior. She is also a talented pianist and has a background in music. Throughout the series, Lois is portrayed as a loving and caring mother who is dedicated to her family, but she also has a mischievous side and is not afraid to stand up for herself when necessary.
